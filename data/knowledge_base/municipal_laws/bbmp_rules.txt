BBMP (Bruhat Bengaluru Mahanagara Palike) SOLID WASTE MANAGEMENT GUIDELINES
Bangalore, Karnataka, India

WASTE SEGREGATION:
BBMP mandates three-bin segregation:
- Green Bin (Wet/Organic): Fruit and vegetable waste, cooked food, garden waste, flowers
- Blue Bin (Dry/Recyclable): Paper, cardboard, plastic, metal, glass, cloth, rubber
- Red Bin (Reject/Hazardous): Thermocol, multi-layer plastic, diaper, sanitary products, rubber bands, carbon paper, broken glass with blood

COLLECTION SCHEDULE:
- Pourakarmikas collect wet waste daily (Mon-Sat) between 6 AM - 10 AM
- Dry waste collection every Tuesday and Friday in most wards
- Bulky items: Call +91-80-22660000 for special pickup

BBMP COLLECTION CENTERS:
- Dry Waste Collection Centre (DWCC): Located in each ward
- E-Waste collection: Most BBMP wards have monthly drives; contact ward office
- Hazardous waste: BBMP Hazardous Waste Collection Centre, Peenya Industrial Area

PLASTIC DISPOSAL IN BANGALORE:
- PET bottles (#1): DWCC or kabadiwala - highly recyclable
- HDPE (#2): Milk pouches, shampoo bottles - DWCC
- PVC (#3): Avoid - send to DWCC only
- LDPE (#4): Bags, films - DWCC
- Thermocol/EPS: Red reject bin - cannot be recycled locally

ORGANIC WASTE MANAGEMENT:
- Bangalore has 200+ biogas plants managed by BBMP
- Apartment complexes with >50 units must have composting on-site
- IISc-developed composting bins available at BBMP offices

EWASTE DISPOSAL BANGALORE:
- Attibele E-Waste recycling park (authorized facility)
- Exigo Recycling (authorized collector)
- Consumer Electronics Association certified collection points in Koramangala, Whitefield, Jayanagar

COMPOSTING SUBSIDY:
BBMP provides subsidy for composting bins: Rs. 500 for home composters

FINE SCHEDULE:
- Throwing garbage in public: Rs. 500 first offense, Rs. 1000 repeat
- Burning waste: Rs. 2000
- Unsegregated waste: Rs. 200 per instance
- Littering from vehicle: Rs. 1000
